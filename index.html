Hana Fubuki: A New Twine Story – In Progress

An interactive fiction project built in Twine:At a prestigious university, MD-PhD student Elara Stone embarks on an apprenticeship under Dr. Samara Haddad, a gardeniatrist who is pioneering the innovative field of plant-based emotional therapy. Their explorations take place within an extraordinary greenhouse housed in the Botanical-Psychiatric Department. While it appears to be an ordinary space, the greenhouse possesses a remarkable ability to reshape itself and its contents in response to the emotions of those within its walls.
